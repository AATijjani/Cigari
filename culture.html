Royal Festivals

Horse riding is a cherished tradition in the Cigari family, symbolizing strength, nobility, and connection to their ancestral roots. It is often showcased during festivals and royal ceremonies.

Royal traditions

The Cigari family proudly wears traditional Hausa attire, such as the "Babban Riga" for men and "Hijab" or "Atampa" for women, reflecting their cultural identity and elegance.
